About Us

We are students from Princess Nourah University, majoring in Multimedia Design. We have developed a graduation project aimed at helping the blind order from cafes easily and without difficulty. We provided them with a menu in Braille as a practical solution to help them experience ordering with ease, without needing assistance from others.

Why did we implement this project? We noticed that blind people face difficulties in knowing the menu options at most cafes, and often have to rely on others to read the menu to them. This may cause them embarrassment or limit their independence. Our goal with this project is to offer a simple and effective solution that helps them order confidently while maintaining their privacy.
